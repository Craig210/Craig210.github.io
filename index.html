2-D

Role: Lead Vocals & Keyboards

Born: May 23, 1978

Background: 2-D is the lead singer and keyboardist of Gorillaz. He got his stage name from the two dents in his head (which resulted in "Two Dents" or "2-D"). His trademark look includes spiky azure blue hair and completely black eyes with no visible pupils.

Despite his vacant expression and somewhat dim personality, 2-D has a beautiful singing voice that has become the signature sound of Gorillaz. He's kind-hearted but easily influenced, especially by Murdoc.

Personality: Spacey, good-natured, and a bit naive. He loves horror films, particularly zombie movies.

Murdoc Niccals

Role: Bass Guitar & Band Leader

Born: June 6, 1966

Background: Murdoc is the self-proclaimed leader and bassist of Gorillaz. He founded the band and recruited the other members. Known for his green skin, pointy teeth, and devilish appearance, Murdoc has a dark and mysterious past.

He's responsible for the car accident that gave 2-D his distinctive look. Murdoc is arrogant, manipulative, and has a talent for getting into trouble with the law. Despite his rough exterior, he's a talented musician and the driving force behind Gorillaz.

Personality: Cunning, rebellious, and self-centered. He's obsessed with the occult and frequently claims to have made a deal with the devil.

Noodle

Role: Guitar & Backing Vocals

Born: October 31, 1990

Background: Noodle is the enigmatic lead guitarist of Gorillaz. She arrived at Kong Studios in a FedEx crate from Japan when she was just 10 years old, speaking only Japanese and the word "Noodle."

Despite her young age when she joined, Noodle was already an incredibly talented guitarist. She's the most mature and level-headed member of the group, often acting as the voice of reason. Her past is shrouded in mystery, with hints of military training and government experiments.

Personality: Intelligent, mysterious, and fiercely independent. She's a skilled martial artist and speaks multiple languages. Over the years, she's evolved from a cute kid guitarist into a confident, badass musician.

Russel Hobbs

Role: Drums & Percussion

Born: June 3, 1975

Background: Russel is the drummer and percussionist of Gorillaz. He grew up in Brooklyn, New York, and was a talented musician from a young age. His life changed forever when he witnessed a drive-by shooting that killed his friends.

The spirits of his deceased friends, including rapper Del tha Funkee Homosapien, are said to possess Russel, giving him access to their musical knowledge and talents. His eyes glow white when he's possessed, adding to his mystical persona.

Personality: Soulful, spiritual, and deeply connected to hip-hop culture. He's the gentle giant of the group, thoughtful and philosophical. Russel has struggled with weight fluctuations and has even been portrayed as a literal giant in some storylines.

About Gorillaz

Gorillaz is a virtual band created by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett. Combining music, art, and storytelling, Gorillaz has become one of the most innovative and successful musical projects of the 21st century.

The band consists of four animated members: 2-D, Murdoc Niccals, Noodle, and Russel Hobbs. Each character has their own personality and backstory, creating a rich fictional universe.

With multiple platinum albums, Grammy nominations, and groundbreaking music videos, Gorillaz continues to push the boundaries of what a band can be.
